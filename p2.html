Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Sugar to balance flavor

Directions

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaf, and peppercorns for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and brown the chicken pieces.

  5. Serve hot with rice.
